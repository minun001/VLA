Takeaway

Detected vehicle context to interpretable ego trajectory prior

This page presents a visualization-level research output: detected vehicle context is organized into graph relations, projected into pseudo-3D context, and used to form a TGCA-based ego trajectory prior. The result should be read as an interpretable prior, not as a validated future label, planner output, or autonomous-driving control signal.
